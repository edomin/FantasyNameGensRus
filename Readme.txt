This micro-programs can generate cyrillic names for your fantasy characters.

How to use it:
1. Open generator folder then open "index.html" then choose character gender and names count then push button. Enjoy.
2. You can also grab dictionaries from "foldername/generator.js" and use it in your project. Drow dictionaries are copyrated by "Wizards of the Coast" and was grab by me without permission from "Dungeons and Dragons" texts.
